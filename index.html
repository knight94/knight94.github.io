About Me

I am PhD scholar (since Jan 2021) and Prime Minister Research Fellow (since 2022) at Amar Nath and Shashi Khosla School of Information Technology (SIT).I am working under guidance of Prof. Preeti Ranjan Panda and a part of the Memory and embedded Architecture Research Group (MARG). Research interest include embedded system, system design and modelling, deep learning accelerators and image processing.

Before joining the institute I was working as technical specialist at Bosch global software technologies for 4.7 years. Primarily working on automotive infotainment platform with expertise in linux kernel BSP, linux video subsystem (V4l2) and QNX. I have completed undergraduate degree (2012-2016) from IIT Guwahati
